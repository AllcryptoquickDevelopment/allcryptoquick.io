Career

We are a startup on a 9 month schedule to deliver our MVP (minimal viable product) which includes a decentralized marketplace, encrypted communication tools, a built in confidential currency and integrations with other crypto-currency projects to provide a use-case for any token.

The marketplace and underlying privacy platform will feature confidential transactions, 2-party escrow, decentralized self-governance and open doors for person to person global online shopping.

We are actively seeking passionate, driven creatives in the following career paths:

  • C++ Developer
  • Junior Developer
FULL-TIME

C++ Developer

This International project is looking for a hard-working C++ Developer. If you are self-driven, dependable and can work on own initiative, then this could be the job for you. Particl Project will offer you the opportunity to work on a variety of projects and with the latest technologies on the market.

The candidate should be able to: design and build excellent and reliable application software; identify and fix bugs; and, work on various projects at any given time. He/she should also be flexible enough to work in different time zones with remote colleagues. Time management skills and detail orientated is a must.

If you feel that this is the job for you and you meet the requirements, please send your CV and motivational letter to:  hello@particl.io

The candidate skill requirements:
  • Solid C++ development knowledge and experience
  • Level DB
  • SQLite3
  • Boost
  • Boost Unit tests
  • RPC / HTTP / Rest
  • JSON
  • CI
  • git
The candidate will also need to have an understanding of:
  • Electron
  • JavaScript
  • Angular
  • Cryptocurrencies
