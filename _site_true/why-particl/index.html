Why Particl

Particl is a mission-focused blockchain open-source project. Our team is dedicated to foster a new decentralized, private and democratic economy,

In addition to being deeply passionate about building great blockchain software and a successful open-source project.

Hard coding the architecture for the new economy

The state of the economy today is not rewarding for most people on the planet. We believe in a different world, without middlemen and intermediaries. In a pure P2P economy the world will be a better, more democratic, more open place. Blockchain technology allows us to do exactly this.

From protecting privacy and civil liberties to promoting open software to build platforms for decentralized commerce, we put our values to work to make the world a better place.

Open Source

Following the lead of Bitcoin, Particl advocates for an Open source policy. Open source projects have positive effects when it comes to help sustaining the success of a project, as It embrace and celebrate principles of open exchange, collaborative participation, peer-reviewing, rapid prototyping, transparency, meritocracy, and community-oriented development. This aspect of the protocol generates the ability for users and programmers to inspect, modify and enhance the source code, and finally build on top of it.
