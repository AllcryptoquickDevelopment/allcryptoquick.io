The Blockchain Privacy Platform

Over the last 25 years the internet has shown us that the only way to secure data is to not collect and store it in the first play. No one is able to secure large amounts of data. Particl is an open-source and decentralized privacy platform built on the the blockchain.

It allows decentralized applications (Dapps) of all sorts to be built within a secure, highly-scalable environment and be integrated directily into Particl’s official user interface. Designing privacy into this framework makes Particl unique. Privacy is one of our core values and is in the DNA of every product we create.

The Currency

The swiss-army knife of cryptocurrencies

PART is a coin with various degrees of privacy to accommodate all types of users and is required to use the Particl platform's marketplace and Dapps. The coin is private by design, offering multi-state privacy (public, blind, anon) and leveraging latest technologies like Confidential Transactions (CT) and Ring confidential Transactions (RingCT). PART is built from the Bitcoin Core codebase and is always updated to its latest version (currently 0.15.1). PART is hosted by its own blockchain and is not a token built on another chain (i.e. ERC-20 tokens).

The Marketplace

The disruptive way to buy and sell goods and services

Particl’s decentralized marketplace is a highly scalable and secure solution for e-commerce. Built with privacy at its core, it uses several platform-wide features to deliver the full suite of tools required to shop and sell products and services online.
The marketplace is built with privacy at its core, meaning that all transactions between buyers and vendors are fungible (untraceable and private). To achieve this feat, many privacy solutions are deployed such as CT escrow smart-contract, IP obfuscation, encrypted messaging and metadata leak protection.

The Future: Privacy native DApps for all use cases
