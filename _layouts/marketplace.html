About Particl's Open Marketplace

The Particl Open Marketplace is a first of its kind market network for the internet.

Using peer-to-peer technology, it operates with no central authority or middleman and is exclusively owned and operated by its network of users; enabling new exciting possibilities that could not be covered by any previous marketplaces.

Particl is also scalable, fast, secure and private. You can buy and sell your favorite product directly for cryptocurrencies, knowing that others canâ€™t monitor your activity.

Features

Private by default In development

The Particl marketplace provides all the benefits of a blockchain based decentralized marketplace, without any of the standard privacy concessions. Any purchase or sale is executed without revealing your identity, just like in-person cash transactions.

Learn about Privacy

No middleman

The marketplace belongs and is operated by its network of global users. Transactions are carried out collectively by everyone connected to the network. There is no central authority to induce points of failure. Third-parties are no longer needed and do not require to be trusted anymore.

Learn about MAD Escrow

Security & control

Transactions are secured by academically reviewed cryptography. Particl gives you control over both your money and data as well as a strong level of protection against multiple types of fraud, abuse, and digital attacks.

Learn about Security

Near-zero fees

The Particl Open Marketplace protocol does not charge you more than what is necessary for it to operate. The processing fees are kept at a bare minimum and are entirely redistributed to its users.

Multiple cryptocurrencies accepted In development

The Particl Open Marketplace is designed to accept multiple cryptocurrencies, not just its native coin. Easily and quickly use your favorite cryptocurrencies to buy and sell products and services and avoid any potential friction when making payment.
